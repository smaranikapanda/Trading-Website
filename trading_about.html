About Us

Sgx Nifty Futures is a great tool for traders to use in order to make informed decisions about their trading strategies. It gives traders a head start on what our trading day could look like. The SGX Nifty is a futures contract on the NSE Nifty. SGX Nifty is traded in the Singapore derivatives market just like Nifty futures are traded in India. The website is updated daily and provides data on the previous day's close, the current day's open, and the current day's high and low. The data provides information on the price of the underlying asset as well as the open interest and volume of the contract. This data can be used to help traders make decisions about when to enter and exit positions.
